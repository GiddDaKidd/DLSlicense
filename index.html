How long will my Occupational Driver’s License be valid?

An Occupational Driver’s License is usually valid throughout your suspension period. If we are successful in court for you, we will receive a certified copy of the Order granting your Occupational Driver’s License this document will be valid for only 45 days. This order will need to be sent to DPS and you will be issued a formal ID card Occupational Driver’s License. Our attorneys will guide you through this process.

What type of license suspensions can an Occupational Driver’s License be used for?

Traffic Ticket Suspensions, DWI Suspensions, Drug Possession Convictions, DPS Surcharges and more.
